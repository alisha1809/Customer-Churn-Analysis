# Business Insights

## Customer Retention

Month-to-month customers showed the highest churn rate.

---

## Revenue

Customers with higher monthly charges were more likely to churn.

---

## Service Analysis

Customers without technical support were more likely to leave.

---

## Recommendations

Encourage long-term contracts.

Offer technical support bundles.

Provide loyalty discounts.

Identify high-risk customers early.
